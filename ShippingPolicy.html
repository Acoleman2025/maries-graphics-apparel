Shipping Policy

Effective Date: June 7, 2025

Thank you for shopping with Marie’s Graphics and Apparel. Below is information on how we handle shipping for all orders placed through our website.

1. Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 2–4 business days.
  • Custom or made-to-order items may take 5–10 business days to produce before shipping.
  • Orders are not processed or shipped on weekends or holidays.

2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your location and selected shipping method. Estimated delivery times:

  • Standard Shipping (U.S.): 3–7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 2–3 business days
  • International Shipping: 7–21 business days (may vary by country)

3. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking details. Please allow up to 24 hours for tracking to update.

4. Shipping Carriers

We ship via trusted carriers including USPS, UPS, and FedEx. Carrier is selected based on destination, weight, and speed.

5. International Orders

  • International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees.
  • Delivery times may be affected by customs delays or local carrier handling.

6. Lost or Delayed Packages

If your package is delayed or lost in transit, please contact us at info@mariesgraphics.com. We will work with the carrier to locate or replace your order where possible.

7. Incorrect Addresses

Please ensure your shipping address is correct before placing your order. We are not responsible for packages sent to incorrect or incomplete addresses.
