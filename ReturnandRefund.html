Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: June 7, 2025

Thank you for shopping at Marie’s Graphics and Apparel. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please review our return and refund policy below.

1. Returns

  • You may return eligible items within 14 days of delivery for a full or partial refund.
  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition as received, with all original tags and packaging.
  • To initiate a return, please email us at info@mariesgraphics.com with your order number and reason for return.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are final sale and not eligible for return or refund:

  • Custom or personalized items
  • Clearance or sale items
  • Gift cards
  • Opened or used items

3. Exchanges

We accept exchanges for size or defective merchandise. To process an exchange, contact us within 7 days of receiving your order.

4. Refunds

  • Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of approval or rejection of your refund.
  • Approved refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

5. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your item arrives damaged or you received the wrong item, please contact us within 3 days of delivery with photos for resolution.

6. Return Shipping

You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was defective or incorrect. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.
