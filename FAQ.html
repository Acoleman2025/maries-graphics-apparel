Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to our FAQ page! Below you'll find answers to common questions about ordering, shipping, returns, and more.

🛍️ Orders

1. How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will receive a confirmation email once your order is placed.

2. Can I customize my order?

Yes! We offer custom designs for apparel and graphics. Please contact us at info@mariesgraphics.com with your request.

3. How can I check my order status?

Once your order ships, you’ll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use that to track your package online.

🚚 Shipping

4. How long does shipping take?

Standard U.S. shipping takes 3–7 business days. Custom orders or international shipping may take longer. See our Shipping Policy for details.

5. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International delivery times and customs fees may apply depending on your country.

🔁 Returns & Exchanges

6. What is your return policy?

You may return eligible items within 14 days of delivery. Custom orders are non-returnable. See our Return & Refund Policy for more information.

7. My item is damaged or incorrect. What should I do?

Please contact us within 3 days of receiving your order with a photo of the issue, and we’ll resolve it as quickly as possible.

💳 Payment

8. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and other secure payment options.

9. Is my payment information secure?

Yes. We use secure, encrypted payment gateways to protect your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
